You are being chased by a trash monster!

In your way, you will find two types of trash: organic and paper.

Drag and drop each piece of trash into the corresponding recycling bin.

If you recycle enough paper, you will build a pair of wings made of recycled paper. This way, you will be able to escape to the next level!

Properly disposing orgnic trash will create compost. This compost will delay the monster.
